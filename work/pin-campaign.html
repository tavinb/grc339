Pin Your Next Adventure

Spring 2014
Client: AIESEC San Luis Obispo
Built with Adobe Illustrator

Working in collaboration with Cal Poly student Taya Arnone, I created a streamlined marketing campaign to increase awareness of AIESEC around campus, and to encourage Cal Poly students to consider an exchange abroad.

Campaign included social media presence, which included various-sized imagery to be used for different platforms.

Legible type and contrasting colors allow the poster to stand out from the crowd.
